Camel

Some Interesting Facts About The Camel :
1.Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
2.When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
3.Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Chameleon

Some Interesting Facts About The Chameleon :
1.Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
2.Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
3.Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they can use to wrap around tree branches.

Flying Cicada

Some Interesting Facts About The Flying Cicada :
1.Cicadas are about the size of a grain of rice when they drop from a tree branch to the ground and start digging.
2.The loud buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorous.
3.They have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
